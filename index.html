The PURENEX Purification Standard

We combine three powerful technologies to deliver the cleanest, freshest water RO + UV + OG

RO (Reverse Osmosis)

Our RO stage uses a semi-permeable membrane to effectively remove dissolved solids, salts, and heavy metals, ensuring the water is fundamentally clean from mineral impurities.

UV (Ultraviolet Sterilization)

The UV light exposure inactivates bacteria, viruses, and other harmful microorganisms by destroying their DNA, guaranteeing 100% microbiological safety without chemicals.

OG (Oxygenation / Ozone)

This final stage enhances the taste, clarity, and overall quality of the water by introducing oxygen (or ozone) to ensure a refreshing and invigorating drink.
